About Me

Hey, my name is Alissa!

As a yoga teacher I hope to support any and all humans on this oh so beautiful and challenging journey of life. I aim to inspire, and allow healing and transformation at their own pace. My classes focus on creating space for everyone to experiment, make mistakes, learn a whole bunch, and hopefully find a way to look inward and explore. I seek to inspire not just the practice on the mat, but also the parts that can contribute to a happier and healthier life in both body and mind.

Training

YogaWorks 200 Hour Teacher Training January - June 2018

I recently became an officially certified yoga teacher by completing YogaWorks 200 Hour Teacher Training in Walnut Creek. I studied under two amazing senior teachers and learned the foundation of human anatomy, alignment, benefits, and risks of asana (poses), as well as the traditional yoga philosophy focusing on Patanjali's Sutras.
