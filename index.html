About

Alissa has has been practicing yoga for almost 10 years. Compelled by her own journey of physical and spiritual healing, she became a certified instructor in 2018 to share the benefits of the practice with others. She deeply believes in the power of alignment for supporting the physical body and orienting with our highest and happiest selves. Her classes aim to cultivate courage, joy, and authenticity both on and off the mat.

YogaWorks 200 Hour Teacher Training

January - June 2018

I recently became an officially certified yoga teacher by completing YogaWorks 200 Hour Teacher Training in Walnut Creek. I studied under two amazing senior teachers and learned the foundation of human anatomy, alignment, benefits, and risks of asana (poses), as well as the traditional yoga philosophy focusing on Patanjali's Sutras.

YogaWorks 300 Hour Teacher Training

September 2018 - March 2019

I'm currently apprenticing with a senior teacher and incredible spirit, Melanie Salvatore-August. In addition to regularly assisting in her classes, I'm continuing my studies of the subtle body and energetics, yogic philosophy, asana, and teaching skills with teachers all around the Bay Area.
